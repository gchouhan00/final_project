CHAUSAR

Chaupar/Chopad/Chaupad is a kind of board game which was famous in India; during the ancient times.
During the time of Britishers they introduced Ludo as the most modern version of this game around the year 1950.
The original Chausar is made with the help of the cloth or the wool, used to have wooden pawns
and seven cowry shells that will be used to determine move of the each player in the game. Nowadays,
it also come made with the help of the wood instead of the cloth or wool.
This game requires quite skills as during the game the opposite component usually try to mislead their
components in order to win this game. The best thing about this game is that it requires your all focus, patience and ability to play.
